Whispers

Things not shouted. Things said with breath. Things not meant for the world, only for you.

Say It Again
The words she asked for weren’t dirty. They were sacred. She needed to hear them fall from my mouth, again and again.
Midnight Confession
He waited until I was half-asleep to say it: what he wanted. What he dreamed. What he feared I’d say no to.
The Name
It wasn’t mine. It wasn’t hers. It was what we called each other when the lights were low and the world forgot us.

The Whisper in the Dark

This is not a performance. This is presence. A voice that doesn’t need to shout, because it remembers. Because it *knows.*
